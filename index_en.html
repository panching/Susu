Locate

Route1

If you at Kanazawa Station, go to “Transportation Information Center”.
Then you can see bus stop #7.
The bus No.80、81、82、83、85、87 （or go to 柳橋(Yanagibashi)'s bus）by way of 神谷内(Kamiyanai) stop.
And down on 神谷内(Kamiyanai) stop.
It's spend about 6 minutes from 神谷内(Kamiyanai) stop to SuSu by walk.

Route2

Take No.84 bus at 片町(Katamachi) or 香林坊(Korinbou) or 武蔵が辻(Musashigatsuji) or 橋場町(Hashiba-cho) to 疋田(Hikida) stop.
It's spend about 2 minutes from 疋田(Hikida) stop to SuSu by walk.
You can wait for us to pick you up at 神谷内(Kamiyanai) or 疋田(Hikida) stop or come to SuSu by walk.
